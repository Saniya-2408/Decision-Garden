Smart Filtering

Only the right tasks when your energy matches.

Focus-first

This feature lets you assign energy levels and durations to tasks (High/Medium/Low; Quick/Medium/Long). The system considers your current energy & mood, then surfaces the tasks you can realistically work on now. Afterwards, tasks get visual indicators (color / shape / size) to show urgency. Small tasks appear as quick flowers; long projects grow into trees.
