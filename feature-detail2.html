Visual Growth

Watch your tasks bloom & grow.

Tasks start as small buds or flowers when they're quick or low energy tasks. As time passes or as you invest effort, they blossom into trees. Neglected tasks start to wilt, helping you see what needs attention. Animated transitions make this experience feel alive.
